Sunday Lunch

A proper Sunday roast, cooked from scratch every week. Beef sourced from Dunwood Farms, vegetables from local growers where the season allows. Three courses if you want them, or just a main if that is all you need.

The kitchen closes at 6pm on Sundays. We do not take walk-ins after 5:30pm, so booking ahead is the sensible thing to do.

Sunday Lunch Menu

Allergen information is available on request. While every care is taken, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
