Getting Here

By car from Nottingham

Hoveringham is approximately 25 minutes from Nottingham city centre. Head north through Burton Joyce and Lowdham, then follow signs into the village. The pub is on Main Street, beside the cricket ground.

Sat nav postcode: NG14 7JR

From Southwell

Southwell is around 10 minutes away. Head south-east through Thurgarton and follow the road into Hoveringham.

By public transport

Hoveringham is a rural village and public transport is limited. The nearest bus connections are from Nottingham via Lowdham, though services are infrequent. We recommend checking Trent Barton for current schedules.

Parking

The pub has a small on-site car park. Spaces fill quickly on Sundays and busy weekends, so most visitors park on Main Street, which runs directly alongside. We would rather set expectations honestly than have you arrive expecting a large dedicated car park.

If you are visiting for Sunday lunch, arriving a few minutes early is always a good idea, especially on fine days when the terrace fills quickly.

SHIO Terrace

Our Asian-inspired street food kitchen on the terrace operates Thursday to Sunday alongside The Reindeer. No booking required. Walk up and order.

Before You Visit

We recommend booking for all dining, particularly Sunday lunch and Friday and Saturday evenings. Tables fill quickly at peak times. Walk-ins are always welcome at the bar.

Book a table online or call us on 07975 884 881.

We have a small on-site car park, but spaces fill quickly on Sundays and at busy weekends. Most visitors park on Main Street, which runs directly alongside the pub. We recommend it as the reliable option at popular times.

Dogs are welcome in the bar area. We ask that dogs are kept off furniture and away from the dining room and terrace during food service.

Let us know when booking if you are bringing a dog and we will reserve the most suitable spot.

A discretionary service charge of 12.5% is added to all bills. This is distributed in full among our team. You are always entitled to ask for it to be removed. Please just let us know.

We are approximately 25 minutes from Nottingham city centre by car, through Burton Joyce and along the Trent Valley. Use postcode NG14 7JR for sat nav.

From West Bridgford or Beeston, allow around 30 minutes. From Arnold or Carlton, closer to 20.

Please let us know about any allergies or dietary requirements when booking or before you order. We will always do our best to accommodate you.

We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free kitchen environment. We are unable to accommodate guests with anaphylactic allergies due to the risk of cross-contamination.

For specific queries, contact us before booking.

Children are welcome. Please let us know when booking and we will do our best to accommodate your group comfortably.

Step-free access is available to the main bar and dining areas. Please call ahead on 07975 884 881 and we will confirm the current arrangements and reserve the most suitable table for you.

The Reindeer is available for private hire for birthdays, celebrations, corporate events and more. The terrace overlooking the cricket ground is available as part of the venue.
