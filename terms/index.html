Table bookings

Making a reservation

Reservations can be made online via our booking system or by calling 07975 884 881. A booking is confirmed once you receive a confirmation from us.

We recommend booking in advance for all dining, particularly Sunday lunch and Friday and Saturday evenings. Walk-ins are always welcome at the bar, subject to availability.

We understand that plans change. If you need to rearrange, we will always do our best to find an alternative date.

Late arrivals

We will hold your table for beyond your reservation time. If you are running late, please call us. We cannot guarantee to hold your table beyond this time during busy service.

Service charge

A discretionary service charge of 12.5% is added to all bills. This charge is distributed in full among our team and is not retained by the business.

The service charge is discretionary. You are always entitled to ask for it to be removed from your bill. Please just let us know.

Allergens and dietary requirements

Please tell us about any allergies or dietary requirements at the time of booking and again when ordering. We will always do our best to accommodate you.

We cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free kitchen environment. Cross-contamination is possible. We are unable to safely accommodate guests with anaphylactic allergies due to this risk. If you have a severe allergy, please contact us before booking so we can discuss your needs fully.

Full allergen information for each dish is available from our team. Please ask when you arrive or when ordering.

Private hire bookings are subject to a separate agreement confirming the specific terms of your event, including deposit, cancellation policy, and inclusions. We will provide this when your enquiry is confirmed.
