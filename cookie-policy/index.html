Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 June 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to remember certain information about your visit. Some cookies are essential for the site to work. Others help us understand how people use the site so we can improve it.

This website currently uses only strictly necessary cookies. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies, so a cookie consent banner is not required. If we introduce any non-essential cookies in future, we will add a consent banner and update this policy before any are set.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These cookies are required for the website to function and to keep it secure. They do not collect any personal information used for marketing, and they cannot be switched off. They may be set by our website host, Netlify, to deliver the site reliably and protect it from abuse.

Analytics, marketing and functional cookies

We do not currently use any analytics, marketing, or third-party functional cookies on this website. We do not track your activity across other websites, and we do not build advertising profiles. If this changes, we will update this policy and ask for your consent through a cookie banner before any such cookies are set.

Managing your cookie preferences

Because we only use strictly necessary cookies, there is no consent banner to manage. If we add non-essential cookies in future, we will introduce a consent banner here so you can accept, reject, or choose individual categories.

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse or delete cookies. Be aware that doing so may affect how some parts of the website work. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, visit allaboutcookies.org.

Changes to this policy

We may update this cookie policy when we change the cookies we use or when legal requirements change. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page.

For more information about how we handle your personal data, please read our Privacy Policy.
