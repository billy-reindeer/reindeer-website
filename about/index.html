The Pub

About

The Reindeer Hoveringham. A village pub beside the cricket ground. A kitchen with a point of view.

Billy Mantle · Chef & Owner

Why The Reindeer

The Reindeer exists for one reason: to serve the very best produce we can get our hands on.

Great ingredients have a story behind them. The people who raise them, grow them and care for them. Our responsibility is to honour that work by cooking with skill, restraint and attention to detail.

Everything we do starts there.

The menu changes with the seasons. The ingredients lead the way. And every decision, from the kitchen to the dining room, is guided by a commitment to quality.

Set beside the cricket ground in the heart of Nottinghamshire, The Reindeer is a place to gather, relax and enjoy food made with care.

Simple in principle. Uncompromising in execution.

— Billy Mantle Book a table
How we cook

The kitchen

Named producers

Every menu is built on ingredients we know and trust. Dry-aged beef from Dunwood Farms in Staffordshire. Fresh seafood from Flying Fish in Cornwall. Produce from Packington Farms. The names appear on the menu because they matter. They are not decoration.

Seasonal by necessity

The menu carries a date for a reason. When something better comes in, the menu changes. There are no signature dishes and no standing menu. If asparagus is ready, it is on. When it is not, it is not. This is how we prefer to cook.

A pub, not a restaurant

The kitchen takes the food seriously. The pub takes itself less so. Walk-ins are welcome at the bar. Dogs are welcome in the bar. You can come for a drink without eating and feel genuinely at home. That matters to us. A great kitchen is not an excuse to make a place feel intimidating.

Hoveringham, Nottinghamshire

The place

The Reindeer sits on Main Street in Hoveringham, a small village in the Trent Valley, 25 minutes from Nottingham. The cricket ground is directly behind the pub. On a good day, the terrace is exactly where you want to be.

No competitor in the area has this. The terrace is not a design decision or a marketing angle. It is the specific, unrepeatable fact of where this pub happens to be.
