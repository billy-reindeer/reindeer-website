Gift Vouchers

Give someone a meal worth looking forward to. Vouchers are redeemable against food and drink on any visit, with no booking fee on top.

£25 Gift Voucher

A light lunch or a round of drinks for two. A thoughtful gift for any occasion.

£50 Gift Voucher

Two courses and a glass of wine. The most popular option for a birthday or anniversary.

£100 Gift Voucher

An evening out for two, or a relaxed Sunday lunch with a bottle. Something genuinely generous.

How to purchase

Call us or send an email with the value you would like and we will arrange a voucher for you. Payment by card over the phone or bank transfer.

Vouchers are sent by post or by email, whichever you prefer.

Redeeming your voucher

  • Redeemable against food and drink on any visit
  • No minimum spend required
  • Not redeemable against events or merchandise
  • Not exchangeable for cash
  • Book a table in the usual way and present your voucher on arrival
