Settle In

Sit, relax and hear the crack of leather on willow. The Reindeer is a village gastropub in Hoveringham, Nottinghamshire, beside the cricket ground. The food is the reason to make the journey. The welcome is the reason to come back. We are open every day. Come eat with us.

What We Cook

Each menu is built around the finest seasonal ingredients available that week. Dry-aged beef from Dunwood Farms in Staffordshire. Fresh seafood from Flying Fish Cornwall. Producers we know and trust, ingredients that earn their place.

There are no signature dishes and no standing menu. If something better comes along, the menu changes.

Lunch & Dinner · Wednesday–Saturday · Set Lunch from £27 · Sunday Lunch from £33

Billy Mantle

The Kitchen

The Reindeer exists for one reason: to serve the very best produce we can get our hands on. Great ingredients have a story behind them: the people who raise them, grow them and care for them. Our responsibility is to honour that work by cooking with skill, restraint and attention to detail.

The menu changes with the seasons and the ingredients lead the way. It is also shaped by how I like to eat: a bit of this, a bit of that, passing dishes round the table and trying everything. Many of our dishes are designed with that in mind. But there are no rules here. Eat however you like.

Simple in principle. Uncompromising in execution.

— Billy Mantle

On The Terrace · Thursday to Sunday

Bold flavours.
Open skies.

Permanently situated on The Reindeer’s terrace, SHIO is our Asian-inspired street food kitchen, bringing bold flavours and relaxed outdoor dining to Hoveringham. Cooked over traditional Japanese charcoal, the menu draws inspiration from across Asia.

Popular dishes include Korean fried chicken with our signature SHIO sauce, teriyaki beef skewers from the grill and a regularly changing selection of seasonal specials. Whether you’re joining us for a quick bite, drinks with friends or a relaxed meal in the sun, SHIO offers a more casual dining experience alongside The Reindeer’s restaurant.
