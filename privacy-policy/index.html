Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Who we are

The Reindeer Hoveringham is a restaurant and pub operated by Billy Mantle, trading as The Reindeer Hoveringham, Main Street, Hoveringham, Nottinghamshire, NG14 7JR.

We are the data controller for personal information collected through this website and through our booking and contact processes. If you have any questions about how we handle your data, you can contact us at:

Email: hello@reindeerpub.com
Phone: 07975 884 881
Address: Main Street, Hoveringham, Nottinghamshire, NG14 7JR

What personal data we collect and why

Table bookings

When you make a reservation through our booking system, we collect your name, email address, phone number, the date and time of your booking, the number of guests, and any dietary or allergy information you provide. We use this information solely to manage your reservation and contact you about it. Our booking system is provided by Toast, Inc., who process this data on our behalf as a data processor. Toast's own privacy policy governs their handling of your data.

Our legal basis for processing booking data is the performance of a contract: you are asking us to reserve a table, and we need your details to do that.

Contact and enquiry forms

When you use the contact form or private hire enquiry form on this website, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this to respond to your enquiry. We do not add you to any mailing list as a result of sending a contact form. Our forms are processed through Netlify, who store submissions on our behalf.

Our legal basis for processing contact form data is legitimate interest: you have made a specific enquiry and we need your details to reply.

Newsletter sign-up

If you sign up to hear from us via the form on this website, we collect your email address. We use this to send occasional updates about new menus, events, and news from The Reindeer. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send. Our email list is managed through .

Our legal basis for processing newsletter data is consent: you have actively opted in, and you can withdraw that consent at any time.

Analytics and website performance

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use this website. This involves collecting anonymised information about your browser, device, and behaviour on the site (for example, which pages you visit and how long you stay). This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.

Analytics and marketing cookies are only placed with your consent. You can change your cookie preferences at any time using the link at the bottom of this page.

Our legal basis for analytics processing is consent.

How long we keep your data

Booking records are retained for up to two years after your reservation date, for the purpose of resolving any disputes or complaints. Contact form submissions are retained for six months. Newsletter subscribers remain on our list until they unsubscribe. We do not retain data for longer than is necessary for the purpose it was collected.

Who we share your data with

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only with the following third-party processors who help us operate the business:

  • Toast, Inc. — our booking and payments platform
  • Netlify, Inc. — our website host and form processor
  • — our email newsletter platform
  • Google LLC — analytics (Google Analytics 4)

All processors are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection law. Where processors are based outside the UK or EEA, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data, where we have no overriding reason to retain it
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where consent is our legal basis)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at or by post to the address above. We will respond within one calendar month.

Cookies

We use cookies on this website. For full details of which cookies we use and how to manage your preferences, please read our Cookie Policy.

Links to other websites

This website may contain links to third-party websites (for example, our booking platform, social media profiles, or local transport information). Once you leave our site, this privacy policy no longer applies and we are not responsible for those sites' privacy practices.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to check this page periodically.
