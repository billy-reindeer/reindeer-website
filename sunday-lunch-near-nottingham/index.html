Hoveringham, Nottinghamshire

25 minutes from Nottingham. Worth every one of them.

For Sunday lunch near Nottingham, most options ask you to stay in the city. The Reindeer asks you to leave it. The pub sits in the village of Hoveringham, just off the A612 through the Trent Valley. Twenty-five minutes from the city centre, 10 minutes from Southwell, directly beside the cricket ground.

The terrace looks out over the pitch. On a good Sunday, with the sun on the tables and the sound of leather on willow drifting across the boundary, there is no better Sunday roast in Nottinghamshire. Not in a city restaurant. Not at a chain. Here.

Free parking on site and roadside on Main Street. The kind of pub you plan a trip around.

Billy Mantle · Chef and Owner

A kitchen that takes produce seriously

The Reindeer exists for one reason: to serve the very best produce we can get our hands on. Great ingredients have a story behind them: the people who raise them, grow them and care for them. Our responsibility is to honour that work by cooking with skill, restraint and attention to detail.

Simple in principle. Uncompromising in execution.

Every Sunday · 12 to 6

The Sunday roast, done properly

Choose from dry-aged beef, roast pork belly, corn-fed chicken or wild mushroom pithivier, all served with roast potatoes, seasonal vegetables, Yorkshire pudding and gravy.

The beef is dry-aged by Dunwood Farms in Staffordshire. The chicken comes from Packington Farms in the same county. These are not generic roasts. The producers are named on the menu because they earn it.

Questions about Sunday lunch near Nottingham

The Reindeer Hoveringham is 25 minutes from Nottingham city centre via the A612 through the Trent Valley. Sunday lunch is served every week from 12 to 6pm, and the terrace overlooks the cricket ground. Free parking is available on site and roadside on Main Street.

Sunday lunch is £33 for 2 courses and £39 for 3 courses. All roasts are served with roast potatoes, seasonal vegetables, Yorkshire pudding and gravy.

See the full Sunday menu

Booking is strongly recommended, particularly in summer when the terrace fills quickly. Walk-ins are welcome at the bar.

Book a table online or call us on 07975 884 881.

Parking is available on site and roadside on Main Street. Spaces are limited on busy Sundays. Arriving a few minutes early is recommended.

Full directions and parking

Dogs are welcome in the bar area. We ask that dogs are kept off furniture and away from the dining room and terrace during food service.

Let us know when booking if you are bringing a dog and we will reserve the most suitable spot.

A discretionary service charge of 12.5% is added to all bills. This is distributed in full among our team. You are always entitled to ask for it to be removed.
