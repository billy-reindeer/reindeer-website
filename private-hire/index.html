The Pub

Private Hire

A village pub beside the cricket ground. Good food, proper hospitality and a setting that does the work for you.

The setting

A pub worth hiring

The Reindeer is a village gastropub in Hoveringham, 25 minutes from Nottingham. When you hire it privately, you get the dining room, the bar, and the terrace overlooking the cricket ground. There is no blank function suite here. The room is the room.

We do not do packages. Every enquiry is different, and we would rather work out what makes sense for your occasion than fit you into a format that does not quite work. Get in touch and we will go from there.

Capacity

The spaces

Dining room

The main dining room is available for private dining. Seated and standing capacities to be confirmed.

The terrace

The terrace overlooks the cricket ground. Available for outdoor receptions and informal gatherings, weather permitting.

Full venue

The full pub, including bar, dining room and terrace, is available for exclusive hire. Total capacity to be confirmed.

What works well here

Good for any occasion

We have hosted celebration dinners, birthday lunches, anniversary suppers, corporate entertaining and end-of-season cricket club dinners. The setting and the food do the heavy lifting. The rest we can work out together.

  • Celebration & birthday dinners
  • Anniversary lunches
  • Corporate entertaining
  • Cricket club hospitality
  • Wedding receptions and celebrations
  • Hen parties and gatherings
  • Work celebrations

All menus are designed with the kitchen's seasonal approach in mind. Dietary requirements and bespoke menus on request.

Larger events

The cricket ground

Hoveringham Cricket Ground sits directly alongside the pub and is available for private hire when cricket is not being played. It is a rare setting for a larger occasion: open countryside, complete privacy, and the pub right beside it for catering and hospitality.

We have had enquiries for wedding receptions, corporate away days and larger celebrations. Nothing is off limits in principle. Capacity, arrangements and catering would be tailored entirely to the event. Use the enquiry form below to start the conversation.

Cricket ground hire is subject to availability. Dates and capacities on application.

Your details will only be used to respond to your enquiry. See our Privacy Policy.
