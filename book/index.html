Before you book

Large groups

For groups of more than 8, please contact us directly or call 07975 884 881. This lets us manage availability and discuss any specific requirements.

Allergies and dietary requirements

Please let us know about any allergies or dietary requirements when booking, or call us before your visit. We take food safety seriously and will do our best to accommodate you.

Sunday lunch

We recommend booking ahead for Sunday lunch, which is very popular. See our Sunday Lunch page for the menu and what to expect.

Deposits

We take a deposit of £15 per person at the time of booking. Deposits are fully refundable provided you cancel with at least 24 hours notice.

Cancellations

We require at least 24 hours notice to cancel or amend a booking. If you need to change your plans, please call us on 07975 884 881 or manage your booking online.
